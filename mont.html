Academic Roadmap And Hands on learning

An academic roadmap is a plan or guide that outlines the courses, milestones, and requirements needed to complete an academic program or degree. In kindergarten, an academic roadmap might include the curriculum, learning objectives, and assessments for the year, as well as any specific academic goals or standards that students are expected to meet.
Hands-on learning, also known as experiential learning, is a teaching method that involves actively engaging students in the learning process through activities, experiments, and real-life experiences. For kindergarten students, hands-on learning might include activities such as building with blocks, creating art projects, participating in science experiments, or engaging in pretend play.
